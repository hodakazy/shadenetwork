BEFORE YOU BUY

🏅 How do I get a rank?

Elite and Shadow ranks can be purchased by joining the Discord and opening a support ticket. The Booster rank is obtained exclusively by boosting the Discord server.

🩷 How do I get the Booster rank?

The Booster rank is only available to members who boost the Shade Network Discord server. Once you boost, your rank will be applied automatically. It cannot be purchased.

⚡ When does my rank activate?

Ranks are applied to your account shortly after purchase or boosting. If your rank hasn't been applied within 24 hours, open a support ticket in Discord and it'll be resolved promptly.

💳 How do I purchase a rank?

Join the Discord and open a support ticket. hodakazy (founder) will handle your purchase directly and activate your rank.

🔒 Are purchases refundable?

All rank purchases are final. If your rank wasn't applied after purchase, contact the founder via a Discord ticket and it will be resolved promptly.

🗓️ Do ranks expire?

Purchased ranks (Elite & Shadow) are permanent — they stay on your account indefinitely. The Booster rank is active for as long as your Discord server boost remains active.
